📖 How to Use Gesture Control

🔧 Setup Instructions:

  1. Position: Sit about 50cm (20 inches) away from your camera
  2. Lighting: Ensure good lighting on your face and hands
  3. Space: Keep hands within the blue ROI box on camera feed
  4. Start: Click the "Start System" button to begin

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Keep gestures clear and distinct
  • Avoid sudden movements for better tracking
  • Ensure hand contrast against background
